A little ghost is being chased by a mean black ghost and you need to help him run away!

Give your ghost a name!

Rules :

  • The ghost can go through the platforms
  • He has the ability to stay up in a certain radius of the platform
  • Pay attention to the black ghost, he will kill you with his bullets or when he touches you
  • Don't go too quick or you'll die!
